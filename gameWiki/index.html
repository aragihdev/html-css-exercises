Welcome to The Undertale Wiki!

This is the central collaborative hub dedicated exclusively to the documentation of Undertale, the role-playing game developed by Toby Fox. Our mission is to catalog every detail, secret, and mechanic of the Underground, serving as the definitive library for fans and researchers alike. Our priority is the preservation of the original 2015 experience. This wiki is strictly focused on the standalone title, ensuring an organized space free from crossover content or confusion with other developer projects.
